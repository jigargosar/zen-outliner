Interaction model

Default: Only bullet dots visible. Clean.
Hover a parent node: ▼ (expanded) or ► (collapsed) fades in to the left of the bullet.
Hover a leaf node: No triangle — nothing to expand.
Two nodes are hovered in this mock to show the effect: "zen-outliner" (▼ expanded) and "Tech stack" (► collapsed).
Subtle row highlight on hover to show which node you're on.
